# Privacy Policy for Pairloom

**Effective Date:** 01 Jul 2026

1. **Information We Collect**
   We collect your email address, names, and any relationship data you provide to synchronize your shared timeline and features with your partner.
2. **How We Use Your Information**
   Your information is used strictly to provide and improve the Pairloom experience, authenticate your account via Firebase, and sync data between you and your partner.
3. **Data Security**
   Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption on Google Firebase.
4. **Data Sharing**
   We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared securely with services strictly necessary for app functionality (e.g., authentication, analytics).
5. **Contact Us**
   If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **appasop@gmail.com**.
